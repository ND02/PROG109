1) What was your previous knowledge of HTML/CSS/Javascript?
Before taking this class, I had no knowledge of HTML/CSS/Javascript. I took this class because it is an important skill to learn, since I want to enroll in the Interactive Media Design major.
2) Did you use a template or did you build your portfolio from scratch?
I build my portfolio from scratch. I had an idea in my head about how I wanted it to look. I sketched out the pages, and then coded them form scratch. This experience was challenging for me.
3) What was your most challenging part of your portfolio (ie, the template, artifacts, CSS, etc.)
The most challenging oart was the contact form. I was having a really hard time with the validation.
4) How taking this class changed your view on Web Development?
This class has made me realize that I do not like the coding side of design, only the design portion. But, I am glad that I learned this skill, maybe it will come to use in the future.
5)What would you recommend to future students taking this class?
I would say make sure you are ready to memorize a lot of tags and rules. This makes creating pages a lot faster. I would also say communicate with your professor. This course has been really difficult for me and the professor has helped me every step of the way.
